Hey, I’m Kayla, a User Experience Designer & Developer based in Atlanta, GA.

As a Georgia Tech engineering graduate, I tackle problems with logic and creativity. I thrive on challenging my own assumptions and gain inspiration from demanding projects. I have a special passion for research in design ethics, as it keeps us grounded in designing for user empowerment.

I love this profession because it combines the best parts of science, engineering, and art. I get to research interesting problems, build practical solutions, and use my creative skills every step of the way.

I believe you should never finish a project as the same person who started it. The gift and challenge of a UX designer is to constantly push the boundaries of your ability to see the world from another’s point of view. I carry this belief with me as a reminder to be humble and open.

In my free time I enjoy reading, writing, making art, and rock climbing. I’m always down for a new adventure, whether it’s real or fictional. If you have travel tips or book recommendations, send them my way!
